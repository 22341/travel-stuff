# London to Porto Train Journey

## Trip Summary
**Duration:** 19 days (Friday 29th May - Tuesday 16th June 2026)  
**Route:** Leeds → London → Paris → San Sebastián → Bilbao → Santander → Oviedo → Santiago de Compostela → Porto  
**Countries:** United Kingdom, France, Spain, Portugal  
**Total Distance:** Approximately 1,400 miles by rail  
**Highlights:** Experience the cultural transition from British to French to Basque to Spanish to Portuguese cultures, traveling through iconic cities, stunning coastal scenery via FEVE narrow-gauge railways, world-class museums, diverse cuisines, and historic pilgrimage routes.
